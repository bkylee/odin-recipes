Protein Shake

Description

I drink this stuff after workouts since I usually have eaten a few hours before the workout.

Ingredients

  • Protein powder
  • Soy beverage
  • Frozen berries
  • Probiotic yogurt cup

Steps

  1. Pour frozen fruits into blender
  2. Pour yogurt cup into blender
  3. Pour protein into blender
  4. Pour soy beverage into blender
  5. Blend
